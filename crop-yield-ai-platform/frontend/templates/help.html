Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Gather Information: Collect data about your field including soil type, pH, area, and current weather conditions.
  2. Choose Access Mode: Use registered account for personalized predictions or universal access for quick calculations.
  3. Enter Details: Fill in crop type, area, rainfall, temperature, soil pH, and fertilizer usage.
  4. Review Results: Our AI model will provide yield prediction with confidence level and recommendations.
  5. Follow Recommendations: Implement the suggested improvements for better yields.
Tip: More accurate input data leads to better predictions. Consider soil testing for precise pH values.

Registration Benefits:
  • Save multiple field descriptions
  • Track prediction history
  • Get personalized recommendations
  • Access advanced AI features
Adding Fields:
  1. Go to Dashboard → Add New Field
  2. Enter field name, area, and crop type
  3. Describe soil and weather conditions
  4. Add last year's profit for comparison
Regional Language: Set your preferred language (Hindi/Odia) in registration for localized experience.

Input Categories:
  • Seeds: Cost of seeds per hectare
  • Fertilizers: Organic and chemical fertilizer costs
  • Pesticides: Pest control and disease management
  • Labor: Planting, maintenance, and harvesting labor
  • Equipment: Machinery rental and fuel costs
Calculation Formula:
Net Profit = (Yield × Area × Market Price × Quality Factor) - Total Costs
Note: Use AI yield predictions for more accurate profit calculations.
